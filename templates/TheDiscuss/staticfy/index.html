About {{ project_name }}

{{ project_name }} is a gathering of students developers.

Its aim is to bridge the gap between experienced student developers and the less experience students.

For the experienced developers, its providing an avenue to share the experience, knowledge and expertise in various fields. For the less experienced developers, its providing a space to learn from the experienced developers present in the school.
